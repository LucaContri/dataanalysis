Scheduling

Food MS PS

Notes:

  1. Days Open = Work Item statuses 'Open' and 'Service Change' excluding the ones with Open Sub Status
  2. Days Scheduled = Work Item statuses 'Schedule' and 'Scheduled Offered'
  3. Days Confirmed = Work Items in all other statuses excluding Cancelled and Budget
  4. Days Available = Days Open + Days Scheduled + Days Confirmed as above defined
  5. Confirmed % = (Days Confirmed / Days Available)*100
  6. Scheduled % = (Days Scheduled / Days Available)*100
  7. Open % = (Days Open / Days Available)*100
